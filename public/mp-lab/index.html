BLADEFALL — Multiplayer Lab

A standalone WebRTC/PeerJS connection test — separate from the game, changes nothing in it. It proves peer-to-peer works across your networks and lets you feel the latency. Open this on two devices (or two browsers): one Hosts, the other Joins with the code. Then move your square with WASD / arrow keys — you should see each other move in real time.
