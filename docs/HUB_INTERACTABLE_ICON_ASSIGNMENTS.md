# Bladefall Hub Interactable Icon Assignments

Canonical set for floating Waystation title cards, interaction prompts, and their corresponding menus. All new artwork should be a transparent 256×256 dark-fantasy voxel emblem with no text, border, card background, checkerboard, or white matte.

| Hub interactable | Assignment | Target filename | Art direction |
|---|---|---|---|
| Quartermaster | Shop / merchant | `hub_quartermaster.png` | Hooded merchant bust with a small gold coin and strapped supply satchel. |
| Keeper | Wardrobe / cosmetics | `hub_keeper.png` | Dark cloak on an iron hanger, accented by a small violet tailoring pin. |
| The Smith | Forge / fuse | `hub_smith.png` | Heavy blacksmith hammer striking a glowing amber anvil. |
| Drillmaster | Classes / trials | `hub_drillmaster.png` | Crossed practice sword and shield over a red training banner. |
| Postings | Quest board | `hub_postings.png` | Pinned parchment contract with a dark wax seal and small quest marker. |
| Your Pack | Bag / stash | `hub_pack.png` | Rugged adventurer backpack with one visible weapon handle and amber clasp. |
| The Mirror | Character inspection | `hub_mirror.png` | Tall gothic mirror with a blue-silver reflected humanoid silhouette. |
| Sparring Post | Training arena | `hub_sparring_post.png` | Straw training dummy struck by a wooden practice blade. |
| Abyssal Descent | Endless survival | `hub_abyssal_descent.png` | Broken stone stair spiraling into a violet-black abyssal portal. |
| Beastkeeper | Pet Yard / companions | `hub_beastkeeper.png` | Armored paw print beside a small lantern-lit beast silhouette. |

Do not merge concepts: the Mirror is inspection, not wardrobe; the Drillmaster is class training, not ordinary combat; and Abyssal Descent is endless survival, not a standard zone portal.
